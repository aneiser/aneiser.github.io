InSignum

This is a personal project I would love see one day become true. I am interested in creating HCI solutions (systems/devices) to make life easier for deaf people.

Why? Because this is an issue close to my heart since my parents have this condition, so I know first hand the problem, sign language and the deaf culture and its idiosyncrasy.

Recently, I have been developing several projects consisting of in-home smart light systems and wearable sensory substitution systems that provide deaf people with the perception of sound in everyday situations through haptic and sight feedback.

In the next video, you can see several situation that hearing people take for granted but for deaf people imply problems. It shows how would be their life with the wearable. You can also see us working on the wearable prototype.

In this one however, we show in an entertaining video prototype of how would a deaf person interact with a in-home smart light system.

More in an academic perspective I also intend to publish a paper in a HCI conference which discusses how research in wearables for deaf people is far from being practically used in everyday life.
