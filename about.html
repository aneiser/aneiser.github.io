What is this all about?

My name is Adrian, I was born and raised in Madrid (Spain), then I moved to Stockholm (Sweden) but now I'm currently settled in Paris (France).

I'm a computer science engineer by the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid. Halfway through my studies I discovered usability and user centered design and since then everything I have been doing has been related to this field.

I have a double masters degree in Human Computer Interaction Design by KTH University and Paris Saclay. As part of the European Institute of Technology program I also have a minor on innovation and entrepreneurship. Topics I'm interested in that made me participate in several startups competitions.

If you want to talk about anything reach me out.
